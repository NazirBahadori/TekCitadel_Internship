Sandwich Lover

Steps to make Sandwiches :
  1. Boil and chop your potatoes. Slice other vegetables as well and keep them ready.
  2. Trim the edges off the bread slices. This is optional but nice to do.
  3. Generously butter each of the 6 bread slices on one side
  4. Now spread some green chutney over the buttered sides. Remember this chutney is spicy so use according to your preference and taste
  5. Now start layering the filling on 3 slices out of 6. Start with boiled potatoes.
  6. Sprinkle some cumin powder over this (you can alternately also use chaat masala).
  7. Now sprinkle some black salt over this. Use this sparingly since we’ll do one more round of this after more vegetables are added.
  8. Now add a layer of onions, cucumber, and tomatoes. Sprinkle more cumin powder (or chaat masala) and black salt
  9. Place another buttered and chutney-ed piece of bread over this and press gently.
  10. Using a sharp bread knife, cut each sandwich into 4 equal quarters.
  11. Plate and serve immediately.
